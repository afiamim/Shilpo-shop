Our Mission

ShilpoShop is a social enterprise and fair trade lifestyle retail chain dedicated to preserving Bangladesh's rich craft heritage while creating sustainable livelihoods for artisan communities.


We work directly with weavers, potters, leather workers, embroiderers, and jewelry makers across 23 districts, ensuring artisans earn fair wages.

Our Story

Founded in Dhaka in 2026, ShilpoShop began as a small collective helping Nakshikantha embroiderers in Jamalpur find urban buyers. Today we operate across Bangladesh, maintaining a strict fair trade charter — every artisan partner receives at least 60% of the final retail price.


Our name comes from the Bengali word Shilpa (craft/art). We believe traditional crafts are living heritage that deserves both preservation and economic dignity for the people who create them.
